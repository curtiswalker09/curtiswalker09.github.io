Rib Eyes and Filet Mignons

The ribeye is carved from the primal section called the beef rib. This section of the animal naturally collects more intrmuscular fat. Filet mignon is a cut of meat taken from the smaller end of the tenderloin.

New York Strip

NY strip steak comes from the short loin of the cattle.

Sirloin

The sirloin steak is cut from the sirloin.

T-bone steak

A T-bone steak contains a strip of the top loin and a chunk of tenderloin.
